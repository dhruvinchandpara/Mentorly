# Mentorly - 1 Minute Pitch

---

## The Problem

**Students today face a critical gap in career guidance.**

Universities teach theory, but they don't teach you how to navigate the real world:
- How do I break into product management?
- What does a VC actually look for in a pitch?
- How do I transition from engineering to founding a startup?

Students are left to figure it out alone—wasting time, money, and opportunities on trial and error.

Meanwhile, experienced professionals—VCs, founders, product leaders—have the knowledge students desperately need, but no efficient way to share it.

---

## The Solution

**Mentorly is a structured mentorship platform that connects ambitious students with industry experts for 1-on-1 paid sessions.**

Think of it as **"Calendly meets Uber"** for mentorship:
- Students browse mentor profiles by expertise (Product Management, VC, Founder, Engineering)
- Book 30-60 minute sessions directly on the mentor's calendar
- Pay per session, no subscriptions
- Join video calls, get personalized guidance, and build real-world skills

For mentors, it's a side income stream with complete control over their time and rates.

For students, it's **on-demand access to the exact expertise they need, when they need it.**

---

## How It Works

**For Students:**
1. Browse mentors by expertise tags (VC, Founder, Product Management, etc.)
2. View mentor backgrounds, hourly rates, and availability
3. Book a session that fits your schedule
4. Join the video call, ask your questions, get actionable advice
5. Walk away with clarity, confidence, and a game plan

**For Mentors:**
1. Set your availability (weekly recurring schedule)
2. Students book sessions based on your open slots
3. Join the call, share your expertise
4. Mark sessions complete, get paid monthly

**For Admins:**
- Approve mentors to ensure quality
- Manage student access via email whitelist
- Monitor platform health and session activity

## Market Opportunity

**Education technology is a $340 billion market globally.**

The mentorship space specifically is exploding:
- ADPList (design mentors): 20,000+ mentors, acquired by Behance
- Maven (cohort courses): $25M raised, $80M valuation
- MentorCruise (tech mentors): Profitable, 1000+ mentors

**The gap?** None of these platforms focus on **university students seeking career mentorship across diverse fields** (VC, Product, Founder, Engineering, Marketing, etc.) in a **structured, pay-per-session model.**

Mentorly sits at the intersection of:
- **EdTech** (skill development)
- **Future of Work** (gig economy for knowledge)
- **Career Services** (personalized guidance)

## Key Metrics to Track (Post-Launch)

Once you launch, track these metrics to refine your pitch:

**User Metrics:**
- Number of students signed up
- Number of mentors approved
- Number of sessions booked
- Number of repeat bookings (shows value)

**Financial Metrics:**
- Total GMV (Gross Merchandise Value = total paid for sessions)
- Average session price (₹500-₹2000)
- Platform revenue (20% fee)
- Mentor earnings (average per month)

**Engagement Metrics:**
- Student → Mentor conversion rate (browsing → booking)
- Session completion rate
- Student satisfaction (post-session survey)
- Mentor satisfaction (post-session survey)

**Growth Metrics:**
- Week-over-week student signups
- Week-over-week mentor applications
- Referral rate (students referring students)
- Mentor retention (active vs. inactive)
